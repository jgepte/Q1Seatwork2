CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Name Pilósopo Tasyo An intelligent man who immersed himself in the ways of science, and gained insight far deeper than those of the people in his village. Despite his intelligence, Piolosopo was often seen as a madman by the townspeople. No one paid attention or had any interest to what he had to say. He is also a widower.
Name Doña Victorina Doña Victorina was a domineering person, she was always commited to do anything gain respect. Victorina was a socialite and friend of Capitan Tiago.
Name Doña Consolacíon She is a brutal partner who berates the ensign, engaging with him in physical fights known through the town. She is also rivals with Doña Victorina but is just like her in terms of personality.
Name Tiya Isabel Tiya Isabel is the cousin of Kapitan Tiago. She is also the caretaker of Maria Clara when her mother had died.
Name Crisostomo Ibarra Crisostomo was a young student that came from San Diego. He had returned to his hometown and establish his own school there after his father had passed away.
